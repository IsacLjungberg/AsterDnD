Bane

A ruthless god of conquest, tyranny and governance.
  • Domains: Rulers, tyrants, dictators, rulership, governance, slavers
  • Titles: The Black Hand (Den Svarta Handen)
  • Symbol: A black hand
  • Followers:
    • Denonym: Banite
    • Cleric Titles: Black Hand

Philosophy and Morality

Banites believed that the strong have the right, and even the duty, to rule over the weak, and that some people are where to rule while others where made to obey. Their's was a philosophy that rejects freedom, believing that it was actually a curse that only harms most individuals, who would be better of under the iron rule of a banite.

They believed that individuals suited to rulership had every right to take advantage of those they rule, and also the duty to use any means no matter how heinous to maintain their control. Banites where well versed in using fear to keep their subjects in line.
