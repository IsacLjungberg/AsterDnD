Lesser Wand of the Dead

This wand is made of dark gray wood with a sharp point, and a pommel carved into the visage of a skull. It can be wielded as a dagger, and in the hands of an attuned creature, it deals an additional 1 necrotic damage to its target.

The item can be used to cast either speak with dead or animate dead. Once either spell is used, neither can be used again until an attuned creature finishes a long rest.

These wands where forged by Zetherian wizards from necrinite wood, seeking to tame the power of the dark god [[Necrigal]], and in this case they succeeded. Many variations exist, some more potent than others.
