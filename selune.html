Selûne

A widely worshipped moon goddess.
  • Domains: Moon, stars, navigators and navigation, wanderers, adventurers, travelers of the night, lycanthropes, shapechangers, illusionists
  • Titles:Our lady of silver (vår dam i silver), the Moonmaiden (Måndamen), she who guides (hon som visar vägen / vägvisaren)
  • Symbol: A pair of eyes surrounded by seven stars
  • Followers:
    • Denonym: Selûnite
    • Cleric Titles: Silvered name (silvrade namn), Moonbrother/Moonsister

Philosophy and Morality

Selûnites believe in acceptance of all without prejudice, that everyone and everything can change given the right circumstances and motivations, and as an extension of this that everyone can be redeemed no matter how far they fall. Change is a core part of their philosophy, and is seen mostly in a positive light, as growth and remaking.

They tend to be somewhat critical of authority, and consider slavery to be among the greatest of evils.

Honesty and Illusion

Selûne is something of a trickster god, and her followers do not believe in total and radical honesty as other deities might. She is a goddess of illusion and shapechanging among many other domains, although she also grants her followers great powers of divination and revelation, among them the power to strip away false shapes. One might say thatwhat the moon maiden gives she can just as well take away.

Powers and Blessings

Shapechanging

Selûne grants powers of transformation, such as empowered wildshaping, shapeshifting, etc.

Revelation

In stark contrast to her gifts of transformation, Selûne also grants the power to force creatures back to their true forms and dispel or see through illusions. Such abilities come in the form of spells like Moonbeam or True Seeing
