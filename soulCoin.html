Soul Coin

Wondrous item Among the worst fates that can be suffered by a soul, a Soul Coin is a large circular coin made of infernal iron, 5 centimeters in diamater and about a half centimeter thick, weighing 100 grams or about a fifth of a pound, inside which is sealed a Soul.

These coins are a primary currency in certain outer planes, but especially the Nine Hells where they are also mainly manufactured. While different souls can hold different levels of value, soul coins are usually made out of the lowest among souls and thus have a roughly standard value outside of special circumstances. The standard price of a soul coin is 100 gp. Existence as a soul coin. The soul in a soul coin is constantly half conscious, trapped and utterly deprived of any sensation, usually in a state of despairy. This is a horrific fate, though not utterly maddening as the soul isn't fully conscious without a proper physical form. Souls later freed who have spent a significant amount of time as Soul Coins are usually deeply traumatized by the experience.

When a coin is actually used for some purpose, like fueling a magical device, the soul is torn into horrific agony as its very existence is burned up.
